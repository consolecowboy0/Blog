# Chart Descriptions

## 1. Network Visualization (network.png)
Force-directed layout of the full Caviar network. 110 nodes colored by community,
sized by degree. Dark background. The few large, bright nodes are the hubs-
the people with the most connections. The small scattered ones are foot soldiers.

## 2. Dismantling Curves (dismantling_curves.png)
Line chart showing network integrity vs operations for each superhero archetype.
The Detective (blue) drops the network below 50% in just 4 moves.
The Bruiser (orange) takes 5. Random (grey dashed) takes 37.
The Ghost barely makes a dent in 55 edge removals.

## 3. Before/After (before_after.png)
Side-by-side: the full network on the left, and the shattered network after
The Detective removes just 4 nodes (marked with red X). The connected whole
becomes isolated fragments.
