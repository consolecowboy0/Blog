# Iran Crisis Timeline - Key Events with Data Signals

This timeline maps major events to their measurable data footprints.

---

## 2025

### March 19, 2025
- **Event:** Rial crosses 1,000,000/$ for first time
- **Data signal:** Bonbast exchange rate, Central Bank governor under pressure

### June 2025
- **Event:** Israel strikes Iran (Twelve-Day War)
- **Data signal:** Rial drops, oil markets spike, AIS dark activity increases

### September 30, 2025
- **Event:** UN "snapback" sanctions reimposed (triggered by E3)
- **Data signal:** Rial hits 1.17M/$. Russia/China challenge legality at Security Council.

### October 12-14, 2025
- **Event:** 52 of 88 Iran-flagged tankers simultaneously turn AIS transponders back on
- **Data signal:** Fleet-wide behavioral shift visible in AIS data. Driven by Chinese ports/insurers demanding tracking compliance under reimposed UN sanctions.
- **NOTE:** This is a uniquely interesting data event. An entire shadow fleet changing behavior simultaneously in response to regulatory pressure.

### October 2025
- **Event:** Reuters exposes Maritime Mutual Insurance Association (New Zealand) facilitating billions in Iranian/Russian oil trade
- **Data signal:** Insurance paper trail linking shadow fleet vessels

### December 15, 2025
- **Event:** Rial hits 1.31M/$ record low
- **Data signal:** Multiple media outlets report Iran "nearing economic collapse"

### December 28, 2025
- **Event:** Nationwide protests begin across 200+ cities
- **Data signal:** Social media activity, protest tracking by HRANA/IHRNGO

### December 29, 2025
- **Event:** Central Bank Governor Farzin resigns (took office at 430,000/$, left at 1.42M/$)
- **Data signal:** 230% rial decline during his tenure

---

## 2026

### January 6, 2026
- **Event:** Rial hits 1.47M/$
- **Data signal:** Bonbast exchange rate data

### January 8, 2026
- **Event:** Iran imposes total internet blackout (most sophisticated in history)
- **Data signal:** IODA, Cloudflare Radar, NetBlocks all register near-zero connectivity. IPv6 routes withdrawn but IPv4 BGP routes left up - "stealth outage." Cloudflare reports 98.5% drop in IPv6 address space.
- **Economic cost:** $35.7M/day (Iran's own communications minister). Online sales down 80%. TSE loses 450,000 points in 4 days.

### January 13, 2026
- **Event:** Reports of 12,000+ killed in protest crackdown during blackout
- **Data signal:** Information vacuum - this is measurable as the *absence* of data during the blackout

### January 18, 2026
- **Event:** Signs of partial internet restoration (whitelisted NIN services)
- **Data signal:** IODA, Cloudflare detect brief connectivity spikes

### January 23, 2026
- **Event:** US sanctions 9 more shadow fleet vessels + 8 firms
- **Data signal:** OFAC SDN list updates

### January 24, 2026
- **Event:** IODA detects initial signs of Iran returning to global internet via telescope signal
- **Data signal:** IODA telescope data

### January 27, 2026
- **Event:** Rial hits 1.5M/$
- **Data signal:** Bonbast exchange rate

### February 16, 2026
- **Event:** Internet traffic at ~50% of normal
- **Data signal:** Cloudflare Radar, IODA measurements

### February 25, 2026
- **Event:** OFAC designates 30+ individuals/entities/vessels in shadow fleet
- **Data signal:** Largest single sanctions tranche. India seizes 3 Iranian tankers. US seizes 4 shadow fleet tankers.

### February 26, 2026
- **Event:** Third round of nuclear talks in Geneva fails
- **Data signal:** Iran rejects uranium stockpile transfer demand

### February 28, 2026
- **Event:** US-Israeli strikes begin. Khamenei assassinated. Internet drops to 4%.
- **Data signal:** NetBlocks reports 96% connectivity loss. Brent crude surges 10-13% to $80-82/bbl. Strait of Hormuz closed to legitimate shipping. Shadow fleet becomes only vessels transiting.
- **NOTE:** The fact that shadow fleet tankers were the ONLY ships still moving through Hormuz after the strikes is an extraordinary data point.

### March 6, 2026
- **Event:** Internet at ~1% of normal connectivity
- **Data signal:** HRW reports ongoing shutdown

### March 15, 2026
- **Event:** Internet shutdown still ongoing (66+ days)
- **Data signal:** Longest internet shutdown in Iran's history
